Watalappam Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup jaggery, grated
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Jaggery Syrup:

In a saucepan, melt the grated jaggery over low heat until it turns into a syrup. Strain the syrup to remove any impurities.

2. Whisk Eggs:

In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs until well-whisked.

3. Combine Ingredients:

Add the jaggery syrup, coconut milk, chopped cashew nuts, chopped almonds, raisins, ground cardamom, ground cloves, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt to the beaten eggs. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.

4. Strain the Mixture:

Strain the mixture using a fine sieve or cheesecloth to ensure a smooth texture.

5. Steam the Watalappam:

- Pour the strained mixture into a greased pudding mold or individual ramekins.
- Cover the mold or ramekins with aluminum foil or a lid.
- Steam the Watalappam for about 45-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

6. Cool and Serve:

- Allow the Watalappam to cool to room temperature before refrigerating.
- Once chilled, slice the Watalappam into portions and serve.

7. Garnish (Optional):

- Garnish with additional chopped nuts or a sprinkle of ground cardamom before serving.

Enjoy your homemade Watalappam, a delightful Sri Lankan dessert with rich, aromatic flavors! Adjust the sweetness and nut quantities according to your preferences.
