Our Story

Mr.Devon is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving authentic Sri Lankan cuisine for over 20 years. Our dishes are made from the freshest ingredients and are cooked to perfection using traditional methods. Our chefs are passionate about bringing the flavors of Sri Lanka to your table and take great pride in every dish they create.

Our Philosophy

At Mr.Devon, we believe that good food brings people together. We strive to create an atmosphere that is warm and welcoming, where our guests can enjoy delicious food and great company. We are committed to using sustainable practices and locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible, and are always looking for ways to reduce our environmental impact.

Our Team

Our team is made up of talented chefs, friendly servers, and dedicated staff members who work tirelessly to make your experience at Mr.Devon unforgettable. We are passionate about what we do and take great pride in providing exceptional service and delicious food to each and every guest.
