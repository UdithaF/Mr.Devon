Masala Vada Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • A handful of fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

1. Soak and Grind:

- Soak chana dal in water for 2-3 hours. Drain the water and grind the dal into a coarse paste without adding water.

2. Add Ingredients:

- Transfer the ground dal to a mixing bowl.
- Add chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, chopped coriander leaves, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and salt to the dal paste. Mix well.

3. Shape the Vadas:

- Take a small portion of the mixture and shape it into a flat round vada.

4. Deep Fry:

- Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
- Carefully slide the shaped vadas into the hot oil.
- Fry until the vadas turn golden brown and crisp on both sides.
- Remove from oil and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.

5. Serve:

- Serve Masala Vada hot with coconut chutney or your favorite dipping sauce.

Enjoy your crispy and flavorful Masala Vada!
